In a constantly evolving technical and regulatory context, it is perfectly legitimate for you to raise some questions. Working at your side, we take account of your concerns and support you in finding bespoke solutions. Here are a few problems you may face:

Data security

Protection

  • Can I prove that the protective system adopted protects me as effectively as wished?

Compliance

  • In the context of a bank audit, do I conform to the Basel 3 standards from my information systems onwards?
  • Can I prove the confidentiality of my clients' information?

Inviolability

  • In the medical sector, how can I prove that my information system cannot be breached with regard to the requirements of the medical confidentiality of my patients (HIPAA standard)?

Electronic messaging

Commitment

  • Can I control the commitments made by the company via the use of e-mail?

Proof

  • Am I able to provide these legally receivable proofs, whoever the employee, whatever happens?

Storage

  • In this context, can I afford the provisioning of the ever growing storage capacity needs?

Information

  • Would I be able to retrieve the right information immediately from among all the information stored?

Investment

  • What investment does that call for?

Virtualization

Optimization of costs

  • Would virtualization enable me to reduce the cost of my concrete physical investments?

Professional efficiency

  • I have just introduced a quality virtual environment. This environment is based in every instance on a foundation of physical hardware. Can I guarantee a seemless management of both physical foundation and virtual environment?
